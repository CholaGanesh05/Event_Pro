Our Process

How we bring your vision to life in 4 simple steps

1

Consultation

We begin with a thorough consultation to understand your vision, preferences, budget, and requirements. This helps us create a personalized plan tailored to your specific needs.

2

Planning & Design

Our team creates a comprehensive event plan including venue selection, vendor coordination, theme development, and detailed timelines to ensure everything runs smoothly.

3

Coordination

We handle all the logistics and coordination, from booking venues and vendors to managing RSVPs and addressing any challenges that arise during the planning process.

4

Execution

On the day of your event, our team manages every detail, allowing you to relax and enjoy the experience while we ensure flawless execution behind the scenes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

How far in advance should I book your services?

For full-service planning, we recommend booking 9-12 months in advance for weddings and 6-8 months for other large events. For day-of coordination, 4-6 months notice is ideal. However, we occasionally have availability for shorter timelines, so don't hesitate to reach out!

What is your payment structure?

We typically require a 50% deposit to secure your date, with the remaining balance due 30 days before your event. For larger events, we can arrange a custom payment schedule. All payment details will be clearly outlined in your contract.

Can you work with my existing vendors?

Absolutely! We're happy to collaborate with any vendors you've already selected. If you'd like recommendations, we have an extensive network of trusted professionals across various specialties and price points.

Do you offer partial planning services?

Yes, we offer a range of service levels from full-service planning to day-of coordination and everything in between. We can customize our services based on how much help you need and which aspects of planning you'd like to handle yourself.
