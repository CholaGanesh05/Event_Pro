Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and booking process

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking at least 6-8 months in advance for large events like weddings or corporate conferences. For smaller events, 3-4 months is usually sufficient. Last-minute planning is available but may limit venue and vendor options.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our standard policy allows for full refunds up to 60 days before your event, 50% refund between 30-60 days, and no refunds within 30 days. Custom policies may apply based on your specific event package.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes! We offer both in-person and virtual consultations via Zoom or other video conferencing platforms. Our digital planning tools make remote event planning efficient and convenient.
